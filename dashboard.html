SQL Query Lab

Queries executed against the sanitized sales database.

1. Show total number of customers Execution Success
SELECT count(*) FROM customers;
Result: 38 customers
2. Show transactions for Chennai market (Mark001) Execution Success
SELECT * FROM transactions WHERE market_code='Mark001';
Result: 1,028 transactions found in Chennai market.
3. Show total revenue in year 2020 Execution Success
SELECT SUM(transactions.sales_amount) FROM transactions INNER JOIN date ON transactions.order_date=date.date WHERE date.year=2020 and (transactions.currency="INR" or transactions.currency="USD");
Result: ₹142,224,545 INR
4. Show total revenue in year 2020, January Month Execution Success
SELECT SUM(transactions.sales_amount) FROM transactions INNER JOIN date ON transactions.order_date=date.date WHERE date.year=2020 and date.month_name="January" and (transactions.currency="INR" or transactions.currency="USD");
Result: ₹25,656,567 INR
5. Show total revenue in year 2020 in Chennai Execution Success
SELECT SUM(transactions.sales_amount) FROM transactions INNER JOIN date ON transactions.order_date=date.date WHERE date.year=2020 and transactions.market_code="Mark001";
Result: ₹2,463,024 INR
